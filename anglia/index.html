Época Victoriana 1837-1901

Época Victoriana 1837-1901

Estilos e influencias: Eclectic, Revivalist, Gothic.

Años: 1837-1901, Reina Victoria.
The straight and smooth lines of the Regency period were opposed with a more oval and circular style. Mass produced furniture had taken a new leven and the Victorian style was rapidly welcomed into the homes of many. Later in the period the demands of mass market would have a negative effect on the quality of the furniture being produced.

Características de los muebles Victorianos:

  • Oak makes a nationalist comeback its Englishess
  • Mohagany and Rosewood mostly used
  • Japanese design shows influence in ebony finishes
