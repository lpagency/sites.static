Cerro Castillo Capital is a leading private equity firm that invests in mid size companies based in Chile. Investments in other Latin American countries, especially Perú and Colombia, are done through subsidiaries of these companies.

The founding partners of Cerro Castillo Capital have vast experience doing private equity and a strong network of contacts to originate proprietary deals and involve industry experts during due diligence and throughout the lifetime of the investments.

The team follows a disciplined investment approach and becomes actively involved in its portfolio companies. Value creation is sought by setting clear strategic initiatives and implementing operational improvements. Significant focus is placed upfront to align incentives with managers and partners behind a clear common goal.
